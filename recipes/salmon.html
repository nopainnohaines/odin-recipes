Pepper-Honey Cedar Plank Salmon

Description

Tired of the same old salmon recipe and want to try something unique yet delicious?

This recipe consists of soaking an untreated cedar plank in water and bourbon, then adding a few creative ingredients leaving you with with a delicious grilled family favorite filet.

Ingredients

  • 2 untreated cedar planks
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¾ cup honey
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 6 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless salmon fillets
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
