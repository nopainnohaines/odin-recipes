Maryland Crab Cake

Description

Maryland is known for it's delicious crab cakes. Filled with seasoned lump crab meat and coated with just the right amount of bread crumbs.

After following along with this recipe, you'll soon be enjoying the taste of Maryland Crab Cakes in the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of crab meat, shredded
  • 1½ tablespoons of dry bread crumbs
  • 2 teaspoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1½ tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • ½ teaspoon of ground dry mustard
  • 1 dash of hot pepper sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven broiler.
  2. Mix together crab meat, bread crumbs, parsley, salt and pepper.
  3. eat together egg, mayonnaise, hot sauce and mustard. Combine with other ingredients and mix well. Form into patties and place on a lightly greased broiler pan or baking sheet.
  4. Broil for 10 to 15 minutes, until lightly brown.
